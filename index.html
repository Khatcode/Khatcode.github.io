Protein 101

Protein powders contain concentrated doses of protein, an important building block for body tissues. Use protein powder supplements, derived from either animal or plant sources, to help you meet your fitness and health goals.

At The Protein Shop, we have something to suit many different dietary needs, featuring a variety of protein sources and delicious flavors.

We carry an assortment of protein supplements, including powders made from the following protein sources: Whey protein: a complete protein, meaning it contains all the amino acids, and provides a source of branched-chain amino acids. Plant-based protein: also known as raw protein, provides a meat- and dairy-free alternative to regular protein powder. Many products use chia, pumpkin, hemp, and flax seeds, alfalfa, quinoa, artichokes, peas, and brown rice to deliver a powerful protein punch. Casein: the most abundant protein in milk, offers slow digestion rates, making it an excellent option for those undertaking endurance and resistance training. The longer digestion process allows for a more gradual, steady release of amino acids to support muscle growth. Soy protein: provides numerous protein compounds all from a mixture of soybeans. Reap the benefits of this vegetarian-friendly protein powder to help support healthy weight management. Egg white protein: harnesses the proteins in egg whites and reduced amounts of cholesterol while still delivering high levels of protein. Keep in mind that these are just some of the most popular types of protein powders. Browse our full selection of protein powder supplements to find the option that's right for you.
